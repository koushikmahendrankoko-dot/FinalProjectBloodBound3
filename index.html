— THE LORE —

A Curse Written in Blood

🌲

The Cursed Village

Your village burns. A dying elder brands your palm with a rune of blood — a curse, or a gift. The line between them will blur before the end.

Chapter I
🏚

The Hollow Dungeon

Beneath the village lies an ancient prison. Something imprisoned here was not meant to be freed — yet the blood rune on your hand hungers for it.

Chapter II
🏰

The Crimson Castle

The kingdom's knights serve a lord bathed in crimson. To reach the truth, you must become a ghost — or a monster. The castle does not forgive trespassers.

Chapter III
🩸

The Blood Temple

The rune reaches its full power. Every ability unlocked. Every sacrifice demands more. The temple whispers your name — it has been waiting for you.

Chapter IV
☠

The Final Realm

The source of the curse: the Blood God himself. You are the weapon. You are the wound. Only one truth remains — and it will cost everything you have left.

Chapter V — FINAL
— CORE GAMEPLAY —

Risk Everything. Gain Everything.

⚔️

Blood Sacrifice

Most abilities drain your own HP. The more you sacrifice, the more devastating the attack. Manage your health like a second resource — because it is one.

🗺️

Zelda-Style Exploration

Explore interconnected overworlds, dungeons, and secret rooms. Hidden items, locked doors, and shortcuts reward thorough players.

👾

Pixel Art World

Every sprite, tile, and effect is hand-crafted in pixel art. Animated environments, weather effects, and dynamic lighting bring the world alive.

🧠

Boss Phases

Each boss has multiple phases with unique attack patterns. Learn their rhythms. Use your blood wisely. One wrong sacrifice and it's over.

🛒

Shop & Upgrades

Spend blood currency on permanent upgrades, new abilities, and rare items. Unlock passive bonuses that change how your blood abilities function.

💾

Save & Progress

Auto-save checkpoints throughout each chapter. Your upgrades, inventory, and story choices persist. Your curse follows you everywhere.
