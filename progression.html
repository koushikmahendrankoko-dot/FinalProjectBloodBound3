Progression Guide

A complete guide to every chapter, boss, secret, and upgrade path in Bloodbound. Recommended only after your first playthrough.

📖 Chapter Timeline

I

The Cursed Village

~20 min Intro

You wake in a burning village. The elder brands your hand with a blood rune before dying. Explore the village ruins, defeat the village guardian boss, and escape into the forest. This chapter teaches movement, basic combat, and your first two abilities.

Rewards: Blood Surge (Ability 1) Crimson Whirl (Ability 2) 50 🩸 Shop Access
Boss: The Village Guardian — A corrupted elder wielding a flaming staff. Phase 1: slow sweeping attacks. Phase 2 (50% HP): spawns fire pillars. Recommended: use Crimson Whirl to avoid the pillars.
🔍 Secret: A hidden chest behind the burning blacksmith contains an extra 30 🩸 and a health potion. Walk into the fire — you won't take damage here.
II

The Hollow Dungeon

~25 min Normal

Descend beneath the forest into an ancient dungeon. Solve pressure-plate puzzles to unlock doors. Navigate darkness zones where your vision is limited. Defeat the dungeon's imprisoned beast — something that was sealed away for a reason.

Rewards: Lifesteal (Ability 3) 100 🩸 Key of Crimson
Boss: The Hollow Beast — A massive creature that burrows underground. Phase 1: emerges to strike. Phase 2 (60% HP): moves faster, begins charging. Phase 3 (25% HP): berserk mode, screen shakes on every hit. Use Lifesteal during openings to stay healthy.
🔍 Secret: Room B3 has a cracked wall. Hit it with Blood Surge to reveal the Dungeon Cache — 50 extra 🩸 and Tier II armor.
III

The Crimson Castle

~30 min Hard

Infiltrate the castle of Lord Vael — the ruler who ordered your village's destruction. Avoid patrols, bribe a guard NPC with blood coins, and fight through the throne room. The truth behind the blood curse begins to surface here.

Rewards: 150 🩸 Shop Tier II unlock The Blood Scroll (lore)
Boss: Lord Vael — Three phases. Phase 1: sword attacks. Phase 2: summons knight minions. Phase 3: reveals his own blood rune — he's cursed too. He becomes sympathetic. Defeating him unlocks the dialogue choice: spare him or finish him. (This affects the ending.)
🔍 Secret: If you spare Lord Vael, he gives you the Vial of the Ancients relic for free, saving 250 🩸 at the shop.
IV

The Blood Temple

~25 min Hard

The blood rune leads you to an ancient temple — the origin of the curse. Rooms shift and rearrange. Blood pools drain HP if touched. The temple is alive and testing you. Four elemental blood guardians must be defeated before the final door opens.

Rewards: Sanguine Sacrifice (Ability 4) 200 🩸 Crimson Crown (if shop purchased)
Boss: The Four Guardians — Fought in sequence with no HP reset between them. Guardian of Flame, Stone, Shadow, and Blood. Each requires a different ability to exploit their weakness. The Blood Guardian can only be harmed by blood abilities.
🔍 Secret: Activating all 4 blood obelisks in the correct order (shadow, flame, stone, blood) unlocks the hidden Vault with a full HP reset and 100 bonus 🩸.
V

The Final Realm

~30 min FINAL

Beyond the temple is the Blood God's domain. Reality fractures. Your rune is consuming you. The Blood God is the source of everything — the village, Lord Vael, the dungeon beast, all of it was his doing. Defeat him in a five-phase final battle to break the curse.

Rewards: True Ending Credits Roll New Game+ Unlocked
Final Boss: The Blood God — 5 phases. Each phase changes the arena. Phase 5: he absorbs your own HP to heal. You must use Sanguine Sacrifice during Phase 5 to deal the final blow. The ending changes based on whether you spared Lord Vael in Chapter III.
🔍 True Ending: If you spared Lord Vael AND activated all 4 obelisks in Chapter IV, the Blood God reveals he is the original villager who created the curse to protect his people. You can choose to break the curse entirely or absorb it — two distinct endings.
