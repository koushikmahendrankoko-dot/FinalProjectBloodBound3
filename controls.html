⚔️ Combat Tips

🩸

Watch Your HP

Blood abilities drain your HP. At 10 HP or below, you cannot use any ability that costs more than your remaining health. Plan your casts carefully.

🎯

Facing Direction

Basic attacks and most abilities fire in the direction your player is facing (last movement direction). Position yourself before unleashing an ability.

🔄

Cooldowns

Each blood ability has a cooldown shown in the HUD. Basic attacks have no cooldown — spam them freely while waiting for abilities to reset.

💊

Potions

Don't hoard potions. The Q key instantly uses one. In boss fights, potion timing can be the difference between a win and a retry.

👁

Boss Patterns

Every boss telegraphs their attacks with a brief animation. Learn the wind-up animation and move out of the attack path before it lands.

🏃

Don't Stand Still

Enemies and bosses target your position. Keep moving, especially in Phase 2 and Phase 3 boss fights where attacks become faster and more unpredictable.
