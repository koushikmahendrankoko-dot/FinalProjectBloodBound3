Abilities

Every ability in Bloodbound is powered by your own health. The greater the sacrifice, the greater the devastation. Use them wisely.

TIER I

Starting Abilities

Available from the beginning. Your foundation of survival.

⚡

Blood Surge

Melee Single Target
10 HP

Channel blood into your fist and deliver a powerful forward strike. Deals 2.5x normal damage in a single burst. Short cooldown of 1.5 seconds. Your bread-and-butter blood ability — reliable, efficient, and quick.

Damage2.5× base
HP Cost10
Cooldown1.5s
RangeMelee
💡 Tip: Blood Surge synergizes well with the Vial of the Ancients relic, reducing its cost to just 8 HP per cast.
Key: 1
🌀

Crimson Whirl

Spin Attack Area of Effect
15 HP

Spin in place, releasing blood energy in a full 360° arc. Hits all enemies within a short radius. Excellent for crowd control when surrounded. The spin lasts 0.5 seconds and can hit the same enemy multiple times.

Damage1.8× base
HP Cost15
Cooldown3s
RangeShort AoE
💡 Tip: Crimson Whirl briefly staggers lighter enemies, giving you a moment to reposition or heal.
Key: 2
🩸

Lifesteal

Sustain Melee
5 HP

Strike an enemy and absorb their life force. Deals 1.2× base damage and restores HP equal to 50% of the damage dealt. Cheapest blood ability in your arsenal — ideal for sustained fights where you need to recover health mid-battle.

Damage1.2× base
HP Cost5
Cooldown2s
Heal50% dmg dealt
💡 Tip: Against bosses with frequent openings, spamming Lifesteal can actually sustain your HP better than potions in Phase 1.
Key: 3 (Ch. I)
TIER II

Unlockable Abilities

Purchasable in the shop. Unlocked after Chapter I or later.

🔥

Hemorrhage

AreaDamage Over Time
20HP

Ignite your blood and erupt in a burning aura for 3 seconds. Nearby enemies take continuous fire damage. Great for holding choke points or dealing sustained damage to slow-moving bosses.

Damage8 dmg/tick
HP Cost20
Cooldown5s
Duration3s
💡 Tip: Hemorrhage is especially effective in Chapter II's tight dungeon corridors.
Unlock: Shop (200 🩸)
TIER III

Advanced Abilities

Unlocked via shop after Chapter III. High cost, devastating power.

💀

Death Mark

DebuffSingle Target
25HP

Brand a target with a blood rune. They take double damage from all sources for 8 seconds. The mark is visible as a glowing red symbol above the enemy. Perfect for boss fights — apply before using your heaviest abilities.

Damage Amp2×
HP Cost25
Cooldown8s
Duration8s
💡 Tip: Combo: Death Mark → Blood Tide = nearly guaranteed kill on non-boss enemies.
Unlock: Shop (350 🩸) — Ch. III required
TIER IV

Forbidden Abilities

Unlocked in Chapter IV. These abilities are dangerous — to your enemies and yourself.

🌊

Blood Tide

ProjectileScreen-wide
30HP

Send a massive wave of crimson energy across the entire screen. Pierces all enemies in its path. The most powerful single-target and AoE ability in the game. The screen flashes red when cast, and you briefly lose control during the animation.

Damage5× base
HP Cost30
Cooldown10s
PiercesAll enemies
💡 Tip: Do not use Blood Tide if below 35 HP. The recoil animation leaves you vulnerable for 1.5 seconds.
Unlock: Shop (500 🩸) — Ch. IV required
☠

Sanguine Sacrifice

UltimateSelf-Damage
50HP

The final ability. Sacrifice 50 HP to deal damage equal to 3× the HP you sacrificed to all enemies on screen. A calculated risk — but with the Crimson Crown relic equipped below 25% HP, this becomes the most devastating move in the game.

Damage150 flat
HP Cost50
Cooldown15s
AoEFull screen
💡 Tip: In the final boss fight, this is essential. Save it for Phase 3.
Unlock: Automatic — Chapter V
